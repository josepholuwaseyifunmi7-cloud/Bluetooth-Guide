Bluetooth Connection Guide

A smart step-by-step system to connect your earphones

Step 1: Turn on Bluetooth

Go to your phone settings and activate Bluetooth.

Step 2: Power your Earphones

Switch on your earphones and enable pairing mode (usually blinking light).

Step 3: Search Devices

On your phone, scan for available Bluetooth devices nearby.

Step 4: Connect

Select your earphones name and tap to connect.

Engineered for smooth audio pairing experience
